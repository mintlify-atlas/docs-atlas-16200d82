Keeping your photos organized makes it easy to find what you need and enjoy your collection. Galey Cloud provides a simple but powerful album system to help you organize your photos.

## Understanding Albums

Albums in Galey Cloud are containers for grouping related photos. Think of them as folders or collections:

- Each photo can belong to **one album** or no album at all
- Albums appear in the left sidebar for easy access
- Photos without an album are shown in "Todas las fotos" (All Photos)
- Deleting an album doesn't delete the photos inside it

## Creating Albums

Create new albums to organize your photos:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Album Creation">
    Click the **"Nuevo album"** (New Album) button at the bottom of the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Album Name">
    Type a descriptive name for your album in the text field that appears.
    
    Examples:
    - "Vacation 2025"
    - "Family Photos"
    - "Work Events"
    - "Nature Photography"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create the Album">
    Press **Enter** or click the **"Crear"** (Create) button to create the album.
    
    The new album will immediately appear in your sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Album names are required and cannot be empty. Leading and trailing spaces are automatically removed.
</Note>

### Canceling Album Creation

If you change your mind while creating an album:

1. Click the **"Cancelar"** (Cancel) button
2. Or simply click outside the creation area

The input field will close without creating an album.

## Moving Photos Between Albums

Reorganize your photos by moving them to different albums:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Photo">
    Hover over any photo in the photo grid. You'll see action buttons appear in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Move Menu">
    Click the **folder icon** button to open the move menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Destination">
    Select from:
    - **"Sin album"** (No Album) - Move to "All Photos"
    - Any of your existing albums
  </Step>
</Steps>

The photo will immediately move to the selected location, and you'll see a success notification.

### Moving Multiple Photos

<Note>
Currently, photos must be moved one at a time. To organize multiple photos:

1. Upload them directly to an album, or
2. Move them individually using the method above
</Note>

## Viewing Album Contents

Access photos in a specific album:

1. **Click an album** in the left sidebar
2. The photo grid shows only photos in that album
3. The album name is highlighted to show it's selected
4. Upload button will add new photos to this album

### Viewing All Photos

To see all photos regardless of album:

1. Click **"Todas las fotos"** (All Photos) at the top of the sidebar
2. The grid shows every photo you've uploaded
3. This includes photos in albums and photos without albums

## Deleting Albums

Remove albums you no longer need:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Album">
    In the left sidebar, hover your mouse over the album you want to delete.
    
    A trash icon will appear on the right side.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click the **trash icon** that appears.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    A confirmation dialog will ask you to confirm:
    
    > "Las fotos del album no se eliminaran, solo se moveran a 'Todas las fotos'"
    > (Photos in the album will not be deleted, they will only be moved to 'All Photos')
    
    Click **"Eliminar"** (Delete) to proceed, or **"Cancelar"** (Cancel) to keep the album.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Happens to Photos When You Delete an Album?

<Warning>
Deleting an album does NOT delete the photos inside it.
</Warning>

When you delete an album:

- The album is permanently removed from your sidebar
- All photos in that album are moved to "All Photos"
- Photos remain fully accessible and unchanged
- Photo upload dates and other metadata are preserved

<Tip>
This safety feature ensures you never accidentally lose photos. Only the organizational container (album) is deleted, not the content.
</Tip>

## Album Structure and Navigation

### Sidebar Organization

The left sidebar shows your album structure:

```
📷 Todas las fotos (All Photos)
   📁 Album 1
   📁 Album 2
   📁 Album 3
   ...
```

- **"Todas las fotos"** always appears first
- Albums are listed in **creation order** (oldest first)
- Selected album is highlighted with colored background
- Hover to reveal delete button

### Visual Indicators

When viewing an album:

- **Photo count** displays at the top of the grid
- **Album name** is highlighted in the sidebar
- **Colored background** shows which album is active
- **Bold text** for selected album name

## Album Limits

Galey Cloud album system:

- ✅ **Unlimited albums** - Create as many as you need
- ✅ **Unlimited photos per album** - No restrictions
- ✅ **Any album name length** - Use descriptive names
- ⚠️ **One album per photo** - Each photo can only be in one album at a time

<Tip>
If you need a photo in multiple places, consider your organizational strategy. For example, instead of "Summer 2024" and "Beach Photos", you might use "Summer 2024 - Beach".
</Tip>
